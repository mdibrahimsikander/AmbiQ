FAQ's

What is AMBIQ?

AMBIQ is an ambulance calling app designed to connect you with the nearest available ambulance services quickly and efficiently, ensuring prompt medical assistance in emergencies.

How does AMBIQ work?

AMBIQ uses your smartphone's GPS to locate the nearest available ambulances. When you place a call through the app, it notifies the closest ambulance and provides your location for a swift response.

How do I create an account on AMBIQ?

Download the AMBIQ app from the App Store or Google Play, open the app, and follow the on-screen instructions to sign up using your email address or phone number.

Can I use AMBIQ without an account?

To ensure the best possible service and to track your requests effectively, creating an account with AMBIQ is necessary. An account allows for personalized service and real-time tracking of your ambulance requests.

How do I request an ambulance using AMBIQ?

Open the app, log in, and tap the "Request Ambulance" button. Confirm your location and provide any additional details, then press "Send Request".

How quickly will an ambulance arrive after I make a request?

Arrival times may vary depending on traffic and distance, but AMBIQ is designed to connect you with the nearest available ambulance for the fastest possible response.
